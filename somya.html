Sahi aur Galat ki Samajh ✅

🛡️ Good Touch vs Bad Touch (Zaroori Seekh)

Bachon, aapka sharir aapka apna hai. Ise samajhna bahut zaroori hai:

  • Good Touch: Jab mummy-papa gale lagayein ya doctor checkup karein (parents ke samne). Isse aapko surakshit lagta hai.
  • Bad Touch: Agar koi aapke 'Private Parts' ko chhuye ya aapko uncomfortable mehsoos ho.
  • Kya Karein? Turant zor se "NO" bolein, wahan se bhagein aur apne mummy-papa ko batayein. Kuch bhi chhupayein nahi!
