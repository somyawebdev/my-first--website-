🛡️ Safety Corner (Zaroori Seekh)

✅ Good Touch vs Bad Touch ❌

Bachon, aapka sharir bahut keemti hai. In baaton ka dhyan rakhein:

  • Good Touch: Mummy-papa ka pyaar jo aapko surakshit mehsoos karaye.
  • Bad Touch: Agar koi aapke private parts ko chhuye ya aapko bura mehsoos ho.
  • Kya Karein? Zor se "NO" bolein aur turant apne parents ko batayein.

😇 Acche vs Bure Insaan ki Kahani

Acche log hamesha madad karte hain aur sach bolte hain. Bure log humein jhoot bolne ya darane ki koshish karte hain. Hamesha apne bade logo se baat share karein!
