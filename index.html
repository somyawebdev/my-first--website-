🛡️ Safety Corner (Zaroori Seekh)

✅ Good Touch vs Bad Touch ❌

Bachon, aapka sharir aapka hai. Agar koi bura chhuye, toh zor se "NO" bolein!

  • Good Touch: Mummy-papa ka pyaar.
  • Bad Touch: Agar koi aapke kapdo ke andar chhuye ya daraye.
  • Kya Karein? Turant apne parents ya teacher ko batayein.

😇 Acche vs Bure Log

Acche log hamesha madad karte hain. Jo log jhoot bolne ko kahein, unse door rahein.
